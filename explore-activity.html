Beyond the trail

Explore other activity

Not every day needs to be a full jungle trek. These half-day experiences complement your time in Bukit Lawang — from cave exploration and river swimming to village life just outside the park.

Bat Cave

Discover a three-chamber cave system, then swim and unwind at the Landak River with lunch prepared by your guide.

5–6 hours · €20/person · min 2

Village Tour

A cultural half-day by becak through rice paddies, with stops to see tofu, tempe, palm sugar, and traditional crafts being made.

4 hours · €25/person · min 2
