Village Tour

A half-day cultural journey through village life — ride by becak, walk through rice fields, and watch traditional production of tofu, tempeh, and palm sugar.

4 hours (half day) €25 / person Min 2 people Start 09:00

Leave Bukit Lawang to visit small traditional villages in the countryside, with wide rice paddy landscapes and the local irrigation system at work.

Morning · Becak ride

Tour the village by becak

Along the route you will see daily village life — palm sugar, tofu, tempeh, and rubber production, and bamboo weaving for home building. Conversations with local residents offer a direct look at Indonesian culture, just a half-hour drive from Bukit Lawang.

On the route

Rice paddies

Enjoy the rice paddies with Gunung Leuser National Park as a backdrop. Ask your driver to stop if you would like to watch locals planting rice in the fields.

Along the way

Palm sugar

Watch the traditional homemade production of tofu, tempeh, and palm sugar.

Along the way

Tempeh

See how villagers produce palm sugar, tofu, tempeh, and rubber.

Along the way

Thatched roofs

See how bamboo is woven for traditional home construction.

On Fridays

Traditional market

On Fridays, the tour can include a visit to the traditional market.

A fresh young coconut is included.

Before you book

Includes

Guide, becak (tuk-tuk), petrol, driver, a fresh young coconut, and a donation supporting local community life

What to bring

Camera, insect repellent, sun cream

Note

Lunch is not included in the tour price. Minimum 2 people; an additional fee applies for smaller groups.
