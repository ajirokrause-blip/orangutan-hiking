Route 01

Easy trek

A short loop along the forest edge — ideal for families, tight schedules, or a first taste of the jungle without a full-day commitment. With luck, you may spot semi-wild orangutans and other wildlife.

Route 02

Relax trek

A full day on the trail at an unhurried pace — with enough time to reach areas orangutans frequent, plus longer stops for photos and quiet observation. End with a tubing float back down the Bohorok River.

Route 03

Orangutan trek

Our most popular overnight — one night camping by the river, waking to gibbons rather than traffic, followed by a second full day in the forest before tubing back to Bukit Lawang.

Route 04

Explore trek

Three days and two nights across two campsites — deeper terrain where the trail feels less like a day trip and more like an expedition. Excellent physical fitness required.

Route 05

Adventure trek

Four days into less-visited terrain, with a new camp each night — medicinal plants, rare wildlife, and a tubing float down the Bohorok River on the final day. Minimum 3 people.

4 days, 3 nights€240 exc. permit

Route 06

Adventure long trek

The full traverse — five days and four nights, with the deepest access into Gunung Leuser we offer. You carry your own equipment; excellent fitness required. Minimum 3 people.
