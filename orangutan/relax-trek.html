Jungle Trekking

Relax Trek (1 Day)

A one-day trek with our team is an excellent choice for active travellers on a limited schedule — about 6–7 hours at a relaxed pace, ending with tubing down the Bohorok River.

6–7 hours €60/person exc. permit Min 2 max 8 Start 09:00

On this trek around Bukit Lawang, you will hike through the hills of Gunung Leuser National Park with excellent chances to spot wildlife in its natural habitat.

09:00 · Pickup

Into the forest

Your guide will pick you up at 09:00 from your accommodation to begin a one-day forest trek in Bukit Lawang. After a short walk, you will enter the Gunung Leuser National Park area.

Morning · Park entrance

Gunung Leuser National Park

You will continue your jungle trek through the rainforest for about 2 hours.

On the trail

Trek and fresh fruit

Along the way, you will pause for a brief rest to enjoy fresh fruit provided by your guide, while keeping an eye out for wildlife passing through the forest.

On the trail

Wildlife along the way

You will continue along the trail, spotting wildlife as you go.

Midday · Lunch

Lunch in the forest

Around midday, you will enjoy lunch in the forest before continuing toward the Bohorok River.

Afternoon · Return

Tubing down the Bohorok River

As your jungle adventure draws to a close, the excitement continues — your "jungle taxi" on traditional tubes will carry you back across the river to your accommodation in Bukit Lawang, with beautiful views of the Bohorok River along the way.

Before you book

Includes

Professional certified speaking guide, porter, fruit, lunch, drinking water, rafting guide, tubes

Excludes

Permit 150.000 IDR/person

What to bring

Camera, comfortable walking shoes, small day bag, long trouser, long socks, mosquito repellent, sun lotion, swim wear

Minimum 2 people. Extra fee €40 if fewer than 2.

ⓘ All routes require a Gunung Leuser National Park (TNGL) permit — Rp 150,000 per person, paid separately at the trailhead.
