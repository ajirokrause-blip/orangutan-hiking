Bat Cave

Discover dramatic cave formations and unwind at the Landak River — three chambers of stalactites and stalagmites, home to bats and swallows, followed by a swim in clear river water.

5–6 hours €20 / person Min 2 people Start 09:00

Explore three chambers of stalactites and stalagmites, observe bats and swallows, then swim in the Landak River while your guide prepares lunch.

09:00 · Pickup

On the way to Bat Cave

Your guide collects you from your accommodation at 09:00. The drive to Bat Cave passes through rubber and oil palm plantations, and along the way your guide will explain how they are managed and point out local fruits, plants, and wildlife.

We do not recommend this tour for guests with claustrophobia.

Morning

Morning · Cave exploration

Bat Cave

Enter Bat Cave with a flashlight (supplied by your guide at the entrance) and explore three chambers of stalactites and stalagmites. The cave supports a rich cast of wildlife — bats and swifts in large numbers, plus snakes, spiders, and scorpions. Take your time to watch them closely and learn from your guide.

Afternoon

Afternoon · Landak River

Swim and relax

After the cave, a half-hour walk brings you to the Landak River. Cool off with a swim and relax amid the lush greenery that surrounds the water.

Before you book

Includes

Certified English-speaking guide, Bat Cave entrance fee, fresh fruit, drinking water, and lunch (Nasi Goreng / fried rice)

What to bring

Camera, comfortable walking shoes, torch, long trousers, insect repellent, sun cream, swimwear

Group size

Minimum 2 people. Tours for fewer than 2 can be arranged for an additional fee.
