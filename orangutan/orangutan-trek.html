Jungle Trekking

Orangutan Trek (2D1N)

Our most popular overnight trek — two days in Gunung Leuser National Park, a night by the river, and a tubing return down the Bohorok.

2D1N €110/person exc. permit Min 2 max 8 Start 09:00

This is our most popular way to experience the beauty of Sumatra's tropical rainforest. We recommend this overnight trek for travellers who enjoy staying active — spending a full night of adventure in Gunung Leuser National Park, with a peaceful riverside campsite.

Day 1

09:00 · Pickup

Into the forest

Your English-speaking guide will pick you up from your accommodation to begin your overnight trekking adventure. After a short walk, you will enter the Gunung Leuser National Park area and continue trekking through the rainforest.

Morning · Park entrance

Gunung Leuser National Park

You will walk into the national park and onto the trail — the start of two days in one of the last great rainforests on Earth.

On the trail

Wildlife along the way

Your guide will point out animals and plants and explain the rainforest and its biodiversity. With luck, you may spot gibbons, Thomas's leaf monkeys, long-tailed macaques, pig-tailed macaques, flying squirrels, Sumatran peacocks, hornbills, and even wild orangutans.

Mid-morning · Rest stop

Fresh fruit break

You will take a short break to enjoy fresh fruit provided by your guide, then hike for about 2 more hours before stopping for lunch amid the cool forest atmosphere.

Midday · Lunch

Lunch in the forest

During this two-day trek, you will venture deeper into the forest, walking through the cool canopy as you enjoy lunch.

16:00 · Riverside camp

Camp for the night

You will arrive at a campsite near the river where you will spend the night. Relax with a cup of coffee or tea, go for a swim, and rest while your dinner is prepared. Mosquito net, mattresses, pillows, and blanket will ensure a comfortable night's sleep in the jungle.

Evening · Night in the jungle

Stars and forest sounds

In the evening, you will have the chance to play games or simply gaze at the stars. Being in the forest at night is a truly different experience — different animals, different sounds, and a stunning sky full of stars. Your sweet dreams will be accompanied by the sounds of the forest.

Day 2

Morning · At camp

Wake to the forest

You will wake to the sounds of monkeys, birds, insects, and the babbling of the Bohorok River. You will have a great chance to spot Sumatran monitor lizards, turtles, and colourful river butterflies around the campsite.

After breakfast

Hike or waterfall

After breakfast, you can choose to go on another 3-hour hike or simply relax at the stunning waterfall near the campsite.

Midday

Light lunch

After a light lunch, it is time to head back to Bukit Lawang.

Afternoon · Return

Tubing down the Bohorok River

More excitement awaits as your "jungle taxi" carries you back on a traditional tubing trip, with stunning views along the Bohorok River.

Before you book

Includes

Professional certified speaking guide, porter, fruit, lunch 2x, dinner & breakfast, drinking water, rafting guide, tubes

Excludes

Camera, comfortable walking shoes, backpack, long trouser, long socks, change of clothes, towel, sandals / flip flops, toilet paper, mosquito repellent, sun lotion, swim wear

Minimum 2 people. Extra fee €80 if fewer than 2.
